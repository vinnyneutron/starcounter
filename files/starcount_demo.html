How many stars are in your sky tonight?

Drop in a photo of the night sky. Starcount subtracts the light-pollution background, hunts for point sources, and gives your sky a darkness score. No upload leaves this page — everything runs locally.

Pipeline: grayscale → background estimation (coarse median grid) → subtract → robust σ from median absolute deviation → threshold at 5σ → connected-component labeling → size/shape filter → centroid & count. The production version uses photutils.DAOStarFinder server-side for matched-filter detection.
